about the Faux Studio

The Faux Studio provides a wide range of residential artistic services for the Philadelphia and Allentown regions. We are passionate about adding creativity and beauty to your home or business, and social or life events. Our services include custom canvas paintings, murals and wall finishes, furniture finishing and upcycling, floral and event design, color consulting, face painting and more. Our primary goal with every project is to exceed your expectations with creative, quality, timely, and affordable services. We have been honored to serve clients across the southeastern Pennsylvania region for over 14 years, and would love to serve you in any way we can.
