Faux Studio Custom Wall Finishes

Custom wall finishes have become an increasingly popular way to add beauty, art, life and creativity to home and office spaces. We offer a wide variety of options from the very popular venetian plaster, lusterstone, rustic stone, reclaimed wood pallet walls, modellos, and much more. We often combine our custom wall finishes with hand painted wall murals as well.
