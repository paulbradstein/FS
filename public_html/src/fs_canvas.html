Faux Studio Custom Canvas and Murals

Enjoy the art, beauty, and portability of our custom canvas paintings. We love to match your dream and vision for your home or office space with custom paintings. Whether nature inspired, a replica of something you have seen, or a rendering from a favorite photograph, we will make that vision a reality. Canvas paintings are timeless, flexible and portable.

Wall murals in your home can make a statement, create an ambiance, or tell a story to those who enter. Wall murals can be combined with our custom wall finishes for maximum impact. We love to bring dreams to life with custom, hand painted wall murals. Contact us at (215) 896-7347 or deb@faux-studio.com to discuss your ideas.
